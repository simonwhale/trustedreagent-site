Gmail

  1. Open Gmail → Settings (cog icon, top right) → See all settings
  2. In the General tab, scroll to Signature
  3. Click the image icon in the signature toolbar (Insert image)
  4. Upload the PNG you downloaded above
  5. Save changes at the bottom of the page

Outlook (desktop)

  1. File → Options → Mail → Signatures
  2. Select your signature, then click the image icon
  3. Browse to the PNG you downloaded
  4. OK out

Apple Mail

  1. Mail → Settings → Signatures
  2. Drag the PNG straight into the signature box
Optional — make the badge clickable. After inserting the image, highlight it and add a hyperlink to https://thetrustedagent.co.uk. Anyone who clicks it lands on the awards page and can verify your status.
