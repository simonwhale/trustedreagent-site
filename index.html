The Method

How We Measure Trust

The Trusted Agent programme is powered by ViewAgents, which aggregates review data from Google, Trustpilot, Facebook, Feefo, Yell, AllAgents, ESTAS and more. We score every estate agent office in the UK across multiple dimensions:

Review Quality

Google star rating, 5-star percentage, 1-star percentage

Client Engagement

Reply rate, reply quality, consistency

Volume of Evidence

Total reviews, multi-platform presence

Market Performance
