FAQ

Common Questions

What's included in each ticket tier?

Day Pass includes full conference access, all keynotes and panels, exhibition hall, networking breaks, and lunch. Day + Evening adds the gala dinner, awards ceremony, and after-party. VIP (Gold) includes everything plus priority seating, exclusive VIP coaching with Josh Phegan, and a VIP gift bag.

Are prices inclusive of VAT?

All ticket prices shown are exclusive of VAT. VAT at 20% will be added at checkout. A VAT invoice will be issued automatically after purchase.

Do RAN members get a special rate?

Yes. RAN members receive one complimentary VIP ticket per member brand. Additional colleague tickets are available at exclusive discounted rates. Check your email for your personal RSVP link.

When does early bird pricing end?

Early bird pricing is available for a limited time only. We'll announce the deadline via email — book early to secure the best price.

Is there parking at ICC Wales?

Yes, ICC Wales has free on-site parking for all delegates. The venue is located just off the M4, making it easily accessible from across England and Wales.

Can I attend just the golf day & night?

The golf day & night on 6th July is a separate event at Celtic Manor Resort. Dinner-only tickets are available at £99 ex VAT for those who want to join the networking without playing. Contact us for full golf day & night packages.
